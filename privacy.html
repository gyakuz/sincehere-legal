隐私政策
SinceHere — 你的智能旅行同伴
生效日期：2026 年 3 月 6 日

感谢您选择 SinceHere（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策将向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。

一、信息收集与使用

1.1 本地存储的数据

本应用采用本地优先（Local-First）架构，您的核心数据均存储在您设备本地的 SQLite 数据库中，包括但不限于：

  • 旅行计划、行程安排、打包清单
  • 消费记录、预算数据
  • 证件信息（护照、签证、身份证等）
  • 旅行笔记、文档
  • 应用设置与偏好

这些数据不会自动上传到任何服务器。

1.2 Apple 登录

本应用支持"Sign in with Apple"登录。当您选择使用时，我们仅获取 Apple 提供的用户标识符（User ID），不会获取您的真实姓名或邮箱地址（除非您主动选择共享）。

1.3 API Key

本应用的 AI 功能（如智能行程生成、语音记账解析）需要您自行提供智谱 AI 的 API Key。该 Key：

  • 仅存储在您设备本地的 SQLite 数据库中
  • 仅用于从您的设备直接调用智谱 AI 服务
  • 不会传输至本应用的任何服务器

1.4 设备权限

本应用可能请求以下设备权限，均在您首次使用相关功能时才会弹出请求：

  • 相机 — 用于 OCR 拍照识别证件信息
  • 麦克风 — 用于语音记账功能
  • 相册 — 用于从相册选择图片进行 OCR 识别
  • 语音识别 — 用于将语音转换为文字

您可以随时在 iOS 系统设置中撤销这些权限。

二、第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

  • 智谱 AI (bigmodel.cn) — AI 智能功能（使用您自行提供的 API Key）
  • RevenueCat — 订阅与内购管理
  • Apple (StoreKit / Sign in with Apple) — 应用内购买与登录
  • MapTiler / OpenStreetMap — 地图服务

这些第三方服务有各自的隐私政策，建议您查阅了解。

三、数据存储与安全

  • 所有用户数据均存储在您的设备本地
  • 数据导出/备份功能生成的 .zip 文件由您自行保管和传输
  • 我们不运营任何用于存储用户数据的云服务器
  • 网络通信均通过 HTTPS 加密传输

四、数据共享

我们不会将您的个人数据出售、出租或交换给任何第三方。您的数据仅在以下情况被访问：

  • 您主动使用导出/分享功能
  • 您主动调用 AI 功能时，相关请求内容发送至智谱 AI
  • 订阅购买信息由 Apple 和 RevenueCat 处理

五、儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

六、您的权利

您可以随时：

  • 通过本应用的"数据管理"功能导出您的所有数据
  • 删除本应用以清除所有本地数据
  • 在 iOS 系统设置中管理或撤销应用权限
  • 在 iOS 设置 > Apple ID > 订阅中管理您的订阅

七、隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本页面发布，并更新"生效日期"。建议您定期查阅。

Privacy Policy
SinceHere — Your Smart Travel Companion
Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Thank you for choosing SinceHere (hereinafter "the App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Locally Stored Data

This App adopts a Local-First architecture. Your core data is stored in a SQLite database on your device, including but not limited to:

  • Travel plans, itineraries, and packing lists
  • Expense records and budget data
  • Document information (passports, visas, ID cards, etc.)
  • Travel notes and documents
  • App settings and preferences

This data is not automatically uploaded to any server.

1.2 Apple Sign In

This App supports "Sign in with Apple." When you choose to use it, we only receive the User ID provided by Apple. We do not access your real name or email address unless you voluntarily choose to share them.

1.3 API Key

The AI features of this App (such as intelligent trip generation and voice expense parsing) require you to provide your own Zhipu AI API Key. This Key:

  • Is stored only in the local SQLite database on your device
  • Is used only to call Zhipu AI services directly from your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server operated by this App

1.4 Device Permissions

This App may request the following device permissions, which are only prompted when you first use the relevant feature:

  • Camera — For OCR document scanning
  • Microphone — For voice expense recording
  • Photo Library — For selecting images for OCR recognition
  • Speech Recognition — For converting speech to text

You can revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

2. Third-Party Services

This App uses the following third-party services:

  • Zhipu AI (bigmodel.cn) — AI features (using your own API Key)
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Apple (StoreKit / Sign in with Apple) — In-app purchases and authentication
  • MapTiler / OpenStreetMap — Map services

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • Exported .zip backup files are managed and transferred by you
  • We do not operate any cloud servers for storing user data
  • All network communications are encrypted via HTTPS

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Your data is only accessed when:

  • You actively use the export/share feature
  • You use AI features, which send request content to Zhipu AI
  • Subscription purchase information is processed by Apple and RevenueCat

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Export all your data via the "Data Management" feature in the App
  • Delete the App to erase all local data
  • Manage or revoke app permissions in iOS Settings
  • Manage your subscription in iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date." We encourage you to review it periodically.
